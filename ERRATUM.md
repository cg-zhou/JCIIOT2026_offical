# Erratum on SOP Materials

We identified several inconsistencies in the published SOP materials, including incorrect station index references and terminology usage.

Although some local wording in the original version is inconsistent with the overall description, the correct target can still be determined by cross-checking the task figure and context.

---

## Affected Cases and Corrections

### Case 2

#### 1) Step 1.3 - Work Area Confirmation

**Original**
> Visually inspect the area around Pick Station 2.

**Corrected**
> Visually inspect the area around Pick Station 1.

#### 2) 4.3 Phase Three: Material Transfer and Transportation  
**Operation Steps**  
**Step 3.1 - Transportation Path Planning**

**Original**
> Access factory map data.  
> Calculate the optimal path from Pick Station 2 to Place Station 3.

**Corrected**
> Access factory map data.  
> Calculate the optimal path from Pick Station 1 to Place Station 3.



---

### Case 3

#### Prompt correction

**Original**
> Please follow the SOP. The object is a blue material transfer bin. The Pick Station is Pick Station 1, and the Place Station is Place Station 2.

**Corrected**
> Please follow the SOP. The object is a blue material transfer bin. The Pick Station is Placement Point 1, and the Place Station is Place Station 2.
